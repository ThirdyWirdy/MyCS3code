How You Can Help

Educacy believes that no child should be denied the right to learn, simply because of where they were born or the circumstances they face. Education poverty is a global crisis, but together we can solve this issue. Now, we would like to invite you to be part of the movement, and help us through your support. Below are some ways you can support our cause, and help the lives of many.

Donating to Action Education

Support Action Education, which has spent 40+ years defending access to quality education for vulnerable communities.

Donate

Donating to EduGuard Insights

Contribute to EduGuard Insights to help fund schools, train teachers in remote areas, and provide essential learning materials.

Donate

Donating to the Access Project

Back The Access Project in order to help in providing under-resourced youth with tutoring, mentoring, and enrichment programs.

Donate

Aside from donating and supporting these programs, you can also support us by spreading awareness through social media and by helping gather more people willing to support our cause. You can also help us by volunteering your time, sharing your skills, or collaborating with local schools and communities. Other simple acts like starting conversations, spreading posters, or donating school supplies, can also help build momentum toward breaking the cycle of education poverty and inequality.

Ending education poverty requires collective effort, and your involvement makes all the difference. By joining hands with Educacy, you become a voice for change and a force for opportunity. So sign up, connect, and take action today. Because when we stand together, we can ensure that every child has the chance to learn and thrive. The journey starts here, and it starts with you.
